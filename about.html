Alkebulan Advisory is an independent art advisory that provides a professionally tailored service to collectors, both private and corporate, assisting them to build, maintain and expand their collections on African art.
With the experience and passion of our team, we are dedicated to providing access to modern and contemporary African art with transparency and utmost confidentiality according to each collectors interest, values and goals.
Alkebulan Advisory is based in the French Riviera in the South of France.
